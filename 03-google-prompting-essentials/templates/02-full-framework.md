# 02 — Full Framework (Task + Context + References + Format)

Uses all 5 steps of *Thoughtfully Crafting Really Excellent Inputs*. Best for high-stakes outputs where you care about tone, accuracy, and specificity.

## Template

```
Act as {persona}.

TASK:
{what you want the AI to do}

CONTEXT:
- {background detail 1}
- {background detail 2}
- {background detail 3}
- Audience: {who this is for}
- Tone: {desired tone}

REFERENCES:
Here are examples of what I'm looking for:
{example 1}
{example 2}

FORMAT:
{how the output should be structured}
```

## Example — Writing an Email

```
Act as a professional gym manager.

TASK:
Write an email informing staff of a new gym schedule.

CONTEXT:
- The MWF Cardio Blast class changed from 7:00 AM to 6:00 AM
- The audience is gym instructors and front-desk staff
- Tone: professional, friendly, easy to skim quickly
- Length: under 150 words

REFERENCES:
Past emails I've written have used phrases like "Hey team," and signed off with "Thanks for keeping things running smoothly."

FORMAT:
A short email with a clear subject line, greeting, three short paragraphs, and a friendly sign-off.
```

## After You Get the Output — Evaluate & Iterate
Ask:
- Is this what I wanted?
- Is the tone right?
- Is anything missing or wrong?

If not, use the iteration methods in [template 10](10-iteration.md).
